# Secrets & Bonus Content — Guide

## Overview
All hidden content, secret areas, warp zones, bonus rooms, and unlockable extras. Rewards exploration and mastery.

---

## Secret Exit Types

### Hidden Block Path
Invisible block staircase leading to a higher route. Hit the block to reveal it, jump up to reach a pipe or vine.

**Difficulty to find**: Easy (visible shimmer)
**Typical reward**: Shortcut, bonus room, Star Coin

### Hidden Pipe
A pipe hidden behind a breakable wall, under a decorative tile, or in an unexpected location.

**Difficulty to find**: Medium (no visual cue, must try)
**Typical reward**: Warp to later level, bonus room

### Vine from Hidden Block
Hidden block containing a vine. Hit it, vine grows, climb to secret cloud area.

**Difficulty to find**: Medium-Hard
**Typical reward**: Coin heaven, 1-Up

### Ghost House Puzzle
Ghost house levels have specific puzzle solutions to unlock the exit:
- Enter doors in specific sequence
- Hit all ? blocks in order
- Defeat all enemies in a room
- Wait at a specific spot for N seconds
- Follow an invisible path

**Difficulty**: Variable
**Typical reward**: Exit to secret level or shortcut

### Enemy Defeat Lock
Defeat all enemies in a room → door unlocks.

**Difficulty**: Medium
**Typical reward**: Power-up room, 1-Up

### Hidden Door
A wall section that is actually a door (no visual indicator, but Mario can walk through).

**Difficulty**: Hard (must attempt to walk into walls)
**Typical reward**: Secret level

## Warp Zones

### Locations

| Warp Zone | Location | Destination | Condition |
|---|---|---|---|
| Warp 1 | World 1-3, hidden pipe after flagpole | World 3-1 | Unlocked from start |
| Warp 2 | World 3-5, vine behind waterfall | World 5-1 | Must have ≥ 15 Star Coins |
| Warp 3 | World 5-8, secret door near start | World 8-1 | Must have ≥ 30 Star Coins |

### Visual Style
- Cannons labeled with destination world number
- Pipes with world-number plaques
- Player chooses which world to warp to

### Rules
- One-way only (cannot return)
- Skips all intermediate levels (they remain uncompleted)
- Star Coins in skipped levels are still accessible later (can replay)
- Warp zones are optional — no requirement to use them

### Gallery
- Concept art unlockable per milestone
- Character turnarounds, world concept paintings, enemy sketches
- Each with commentary text

### Music Room
- Listen to any unlocked music track
- Tracks unlock as worlds are completed
- Shows composer name, track length, play count
- Background: animated music visualizer

### Developer Commentary Mode
- Toggle in settings after unlock
- Small bubbles appear at certain locations in levels
- Press interact to read developer note
- Covers design decisions, secrets, Easter eggs

### Ultimate Challenge Level
- One screen, one life, 30 seconds
- Impossible gauntlet of every hazard in the game
- Completion reward: Gold Mario skin
